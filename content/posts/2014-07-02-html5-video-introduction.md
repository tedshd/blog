## HTML5 - Video Introduction

這是一篇關於如何用 web 建置一個 video player 的文章, 這裡使用 HTML5 video 與一些 open source library 來建立 player

### Some Function

#### Main

* video pause
* video play
* video autoPlay or no-autoPlay
* show current video time
* show total video time
* time jump
* full screen

#### Other

* keyboard control
* mute control
* volume up & down
* buffer handle
* set quality
